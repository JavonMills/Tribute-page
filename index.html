This is a tribute page for the American R&B singer Christoper Brent Wood also known Brent Faiyaz. He was born September 19th 1995 and raised in Columbia, Maryland, United States. He is a Singer songwriter and record producer.

Brent Faiyaz's most recent studio album called Wasteland Release date on 8 July 2022 containing 19 songs.

Brent Faiyaz album released on 7th of February of 2020 called "Fuck the World" with a tracklist of 10 songs.

Brent Faiyaz released a short EP called "Lost" with a tracklist of 6 songs on the 19th of October 2018.

Brent Faiyaz first studio album released on 13th of October 2017 called "Sonder Son" with a tracklist of 12 songs.
